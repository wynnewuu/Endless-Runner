Control the player by keyboard's up, down, left and right key. Press space for jump and double space for double jump. Ctrl for run. 
Coins are collectable for player to collect. 
You can Jump on the top of enemy of destory the enemy. 
There are various potion for players to pick up that allow player to perform powerup, such as teleport skill, increase player's hp, 
decrease gravity, allow player to do laser attack using left mouse, and increase player's speed.
If the player fall below the platform or run out of the hp the game will restart.
Get to the end of the platform to finish the game. Once the player reach the end of the last level a congratulation message will pop out.
Have FUN!!!
